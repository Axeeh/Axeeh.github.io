**Title:** Worldwide Nuclear Energy Capacity

**Context:**
This Dataset tracks the Energy Capacity (MW) for each active and inactive Nuclear Reactor in the world.

**Data Sources:**
The data for this visualization was sourced from the Global Nuclear Energy Dataset

**Dataset Content:**
Columns in the dataset:
- `Country/Area`: Full country name.
- `Capacity (MW)`: Energy Capacity in MW.
- `Status`: Reactor Status.
- `Reactor Type`: Type of Reactor.

**Acknowledgments:**
The dataset and its preprocessing were derived from Global Energy Monitor.
